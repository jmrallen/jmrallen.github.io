Detecting Disturbance to Agricultural Productivity from Historical Armed Conflict in Afghanistan: The Panjshir Offensives, 1980-1985

2024 Remote Sensing / Conflict
Remote Sensing Agriculture Armed Conflict Afghanistan EGU 2024

This research uses satellite imagery to detect disturbances to agricultural productivity caused by the Soviet-Afghan War in the Panjshir Valley. The Panjshir Offensives (1980-1985) were a series of major military operations that devastated local agriculture and infrastructure.

Presented at the EGU General Assembly 2024 in Vienna, Austria. View the EGU abstract.
